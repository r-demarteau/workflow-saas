# Privacy Policy — Teamdock

**Version:** 1.1  
**Effective date:** 15 May 2026  
**Controller:** Raymond Demarteau (trading as Teamdock), Netherlands  
**Contact:** legal@teamdock.ai

This policy covers how Teamdock processes personal data of **store operators** (subscribers). For how store operators' customer data is processed, see the [Data Processing Agreement](DPA.md).

---

## 1. Data collected

- Email address and company name — to provision workspace and send transactional emails
- Billing information — processed by Stripe (card details never stored by Teamdock)
- Usage data — logins, feature interactions, error logs
- Technical data — IP address, browser type (security logging)

## 2. Legal basis and purpose

| Purpose | Legal basis |
|---------|------------|
| Provision and operate workspace | Performance of contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) |
| Transactional emails | Performance of contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) |
| Payment processing | Performance of contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) |
| Security logging | Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)) |
| Product improvement | Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)) |

## 3. Data storage

EU servers only (Hetzner, Germany). Per-tenant isolated databases. Data never shared between tenants.

## 4. Sub-processors

| Sub-processor | Purpose | Location | Transfer |
|---------------|---------|----------|----------|
| Hetzner Online GmbH | Infrastructure | Germany (EU) | None |
| Stripe, Inc. | Payment processing | US | SCCs |
| OpenAI Ireland Ltd | AI features (optional) | US | SCCs |
| Google LLC (Vertex AI) | AI features (optional) | US | SCCs |
| Groq, Inc. | AI features (optional) | US | SCCs |

## 5. Retention

Account data retained while subscription is active. Deleted within 30 days of cancellation. Billing records retained 7 years (Dutch tax law).

## 6. Your rights (GDPR)

Access, rectification, restriction, portability, erasure, objection. Email legal@teamdock.ai. Response within 30 days. Right to lodge complaint with Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens (autoriteitpersoonsgegevens.nl).

## 7. Cookies

Essential session cookies only. No tracking or advertising cookies.

## 8. Changes

Material changes notified by email. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
